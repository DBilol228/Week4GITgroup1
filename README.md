# KD-Group — Agricultural & Farming Truck Parts

Welcome to the official repository for **KD-Group**. We are a premier B2B and B2C supplier specializing exclusively in high-durability parts and components for farming trucks, agricultural transports, and heavy-duty utility vehicles. 

Unlike general automotive suppliers, our focus is entirely on non-engine structural, chassis, and body components built to survive the demanding environments of modern agriculture.

---

## 🚜 What We Do

We keep farming fleets moving by providing high-quality replacements for the parts that bear the brunt of daily field operations. We do **not** supply internal engine parts; instead, we specialize in the rugged exterior, structural, and mechanical components unique to agricultural logistics.

### Our Core Product Categories
* **Chassis & Suspension:** Heavy-duty leaf springs, shock absorbers, and reinforced axle components designed to handle heavy crop payloads over uneven terrain.
* **Towing & Coupling Systems:** Agricultural hitches, fifth wheels, drawbars, and coupling pins for secure trailer transport.
* **Body & Cab Protection:** Mudguards, reinforced bumpers, specialized mirrors, and protective grilles to shield trucks from debris, dust, and brush.
* **Cargo Bed & Tailgate Hardware:** Hydraulic lift components, tailgate locks, tie-down brackets, and tarping systems for grain and livestock transport.
* **Utility Lighting & Electrical:** High-visibility LED work lights, beacon lights, and heavy-duty wiring harnesses optimized for night harvesting and dusty fields.
